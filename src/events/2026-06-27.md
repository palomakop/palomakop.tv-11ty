For the second year in a row, [REALITY ORP](/art/reality-orp), and others from the Weird Music Night crew, are coming to perform at Cha North for Upstate Arts Weekend.

We’ll probably be performing on the later side of this event, since it’s outdoors and we are using a projector. But it’s worth coming a little earlier to check out the other art; last year, they had some cool sculptures in the forest, and open studios for the artists in residence there.
